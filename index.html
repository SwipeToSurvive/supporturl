Game Description:

Swipe to Survive takes place in the zombie apocalypse. The game is a fast-paced first person endless runner game where you have to avoid obstacles to prevent the zombies from catching up to you. The game offers a tilt-mode for higher intensity gameplay where you have to rotate your device to change lanes and a swipe-mode to act as a casual and more traditional endless runner game. Additionally, the player must swipe up or down to avoid obstacles.
